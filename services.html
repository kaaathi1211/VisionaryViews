Our Step By Step Guide

Step One: Consultation

Vision & Goals: Let's talk about what you want from the shoot, whether it's headshots or portraits.

Personalised Experience: We'll tailor the session to match your style and preferences.

Detailed Planning: From location to specific shots, we'll work closely to make sure everything reflects your vision.


Step Two: Photoshoot

Relax & Enjoy: On the day of the shoot, just sit back and enjoy! I'll take care of everything, guiding you through poses and positions to capture your best angles and expressions.

Expert Guidance: Whether we're in the studio or out in nature, I'll ensure you feel comfortable and at ease, allowing your personality to shine through in every shot.


Step Three: Viewing and Selection.

Image Selection: After the shoot, we'll go through the images together and you'll pick your favourites.

Personalised Assistance: I'll guide you in selecting the perfect shots that meet your requirements.

Final Touches: Once you've chosen, I'll edit and deliver high-quality photos for you to cherish and share with pride.
